Dragon-Mandering

Board Game Design | 2013

A group project for my Game Design and Development 1 class. With 3 other people, we wrote a game design document and created a cardboard prototype for a game about dragons, gerrymandering, and possibly destroying friendships. I created illustrations for resource tiles and card backs.

Made in Adobe Illustrator CS6.
