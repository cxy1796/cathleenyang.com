Modern Languages and Cultures Mug

Mug Design | 2012

I participated in a mug design contest held by the Modern Languages and Cultures Center at RIT and won. The winning design was replicated onto mugs to be used as promotion for faculty, students, and friends of RIT. In 2013, the department contacted me to update the design to be printed on magnets for ImagineRIT and to be used as the department's logo.

The company's image of what the design would look like on the mugs.

Made in Adobe Illustrator CS5.5.
